Cranial Nerve Examination

Screening CN examination suitable for most patients

 

I

-        Have you noticed any change in your sense of smell?

 

II

 

-        TEST PUPILS!

-        Have you noticed any change in your vision?

-        Assess visual acuity one eye at a time

·        How many fingers am I holding up?

·        Or use newspaper headline/text to assess near and far vision one eye at a time

 

-        Look straight at my nose. Are there any parts of my face that are missing or distorted?

-        Keep looking at my nose, I’m going to move my fingers, I want you to point to the side on which I move them.

·        right, left then together. If patient neglect’s one side when you move both sides together à visual inattention à ?stroke (parietal lobe)

 

-        Visual fields

 

III, IV, VI

 

-        Eye movements

·        tell me at any point if you see double

 

[by the end of eye movements you have assess nerves I-VI + VI]

 

V

 

-        Sensation to the face

-        Muscles of mastication

 

VII

 

-        Raise eyebrows

-        Close eyes tight

-        Puff out cheeks

-        Smile so I can see your teeth

 

VIII

 

-        Free field hearing test

 

IX and X

 

-        Swallow

-        Stick out tongue and say ‘aaah’

·        uvula deviates AWAY from damaged side

-        does the patient sound hoarse?

 

XI

 

-        Shrug shoulders (trapezius muscle)

-        Turn head against resistance (sternocleidomastoid muscle)

 

XII

 

-        Stick out tongue

·        tongue deviates toward affected side
